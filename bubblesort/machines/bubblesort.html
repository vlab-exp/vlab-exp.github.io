Bubblesort

Objective

In this experiment, you need to step through the Bubblesort algorithm, to sort the given list.

Experiment Setup

There is just one control called 'Next'. The 'Next' button performs the next step in the Bubblesort algorithm. You can observe the list getting sorted as the algorithm progresses.

The two indicators 'i' and 'b', mark the values of 'current index' and 'boundary' variables involved in the algorithm.

Procedure

Follow the given steps to perform the experiment.

  1. Click on the 'Next' button to step through the algorithm.
  2. When the boundary reaches the value 1, check if the list is sorted.
